Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you travel

How do I book a tour with Explore India Tours?
+
It's super easy! Just click any "Book Now" button or WhatsApp us directly. Our friendly team will guide you through the booking process and answer all your questions.
Are the tour prices inclusive of everything?
+
Our tour packages include accommodation, transport, guided sightseeing, and select meals. Any personal expenses, airfare, or optional activities are not included. We'll give you a clear breakdown upfront.
Can I customise a tour itinerary?
+
Absolutely! We love crafting bespoke travel experiences. Tell us your preferences, budget, and travel dates via WhatsApp and we'll design a tailor-made itinerary just for you.
What is the cancellation policy?
+
Cancellations made 15+ days before departure receive a full refund. Between 7–14 days, a 25% charge applies. Within 7 days, 50% is non-refundable. We always try to be flexible — please reach out to us!
Is it safe to travel solo with your tours?
+
Yes, completely! We have solo-friendly group tours where you'll meet fellow travellers. Our experienced guides are trained in safety protocols and are available 24/7 during the trip.
